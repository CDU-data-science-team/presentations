• Scikit-learn is probably the most popular ML library in Python.
• It offers a wealth of functions and classes for (un)supervised learning,
feature engineering & selection, hyperparameter tuning etc.
• It does not have Deep Learning models, but integration with e.g.
Keras is easily done with a wrapper.
• It has several advantages on its own, but also relative to key ML
packages in R like tidymodels and mlr3 . Let’s discover them!

• Delve deeper into the world of ML and get things done fast.
